## The patterns behind every template

Study these before you start writing your own variations.

1. **Specific observation in the opening line.** Not "I hope you're well" — something that proves you looked at their work.
2. **One pain point, not three.** If you list multiple, none lands.
3. **Concrete numbers.** "$500 flat, 7 days, $500–2k/month" beats "affordable, fast, profitable."
4. **Low-friction ask.** A 15-minute call beats "let's discuss." A link beats "reply to this email."
5. **P.S. with social proof** works unreasonably well. People read P.S. lines first.
6. **Under 150 words.** Every single one in this pack is. Yours should be too.
